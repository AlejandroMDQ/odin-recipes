Spicy Chicken Burritos

How to cook this almighty chicken burritos!

Do you like spicy food? You're at the correct place! We'll learning to prepare this burritos easily.

Caution: this burritos will start a fire inside you!

Go back to homepage!

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound skinless boneless chicken breasts
  • 2 teaspoons of butter
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1 pinch pepper
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar
  • 2/3 cup sour cream
  • 1 cup diced green pepper
  • 1/3 cup osalsa
  • 1/2 cup sliced black olives (Optional)
  • 1 jalapeno pepper, seeded and minced
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
  • 4 large flour tortillas

Steps:

  1. Step 1: Cut chicken into small 1-inch (2.5 cm). In skillet, melt butter over medium high heat and cook chicken for about 8 minutes or until no longer pink inside. Salt and pepper to taste. Pour into large bowl and stir in 1/2 cup (375 mL) of the cheese, sour cream, green pepper, salsa, olives, jalapeno, and cilantro.
  2. Step 2: Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (180 degrees C).
  3. Step 3: Divide mixture among tortillas: Fold and roll to enclose filling completely; place seam side down in small baking dish. Sprinkle with remaining cheese.
  4. Step 4: Bake in centre of oven for about 20 minutes or until cheese is melted and tortillas are crispy.
